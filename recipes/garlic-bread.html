Description

This is an easy recipie for garlic bread, which is perfect to go with your spaghetti.

Ingreadiants

  • 1 loaf bread
  • 1/2 cup unsalted softened butter
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 heaping teaspoon chopped parsley
  • (optional) 1/4 cup grated parmesean

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. cut loaf in half, mix garlic, butter and parsley, spread over bread
  3. heat in oven for 10 minutes
  4. (optional) sprinkle with parmesean
  5. slice
