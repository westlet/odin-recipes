Description

This is a breakfast classic, scrambled eggs. Scrambled eggs are easy to make, yet also fast and tasty making them a popular choice

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup half and half
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablspoon unsalted butter
  • black pepper
  • fresh chopped herbs for servinv

Directions

  1. aggressively whisk eggs, half and half, and salt until uniform in color
  2. melt butter in pan over medium heat until butter starts to foam
  3. add eggs to pan and reduce heat to medium low
  4. wait for edges to set, then gently push eggs around pan, pausing in between to let liquid egg settle
  5. when eggs are mostly cooked, slowly fold eggs into itself a few times
  6. remove eggs from heat when eggs slightly shimmer
  7. transfer to plates, finish with pepper and herbs
