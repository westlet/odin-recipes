Spaghetti

Description

This is a recipie for a classic plate of spaghetti, a personal favorite of mine.

Ingrediants

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 can beef broth
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 teaspoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1 box spaghetti noodles

Directions

  1. cook ground beef, salt, and pepper in a pan over medium-high until no longer pink, drain
  2. add beef broth, tomato sauce, tomato paste, garlic, sugar, and italian seasoning.stir until well mixed
  3. add finished noodles to sauce and mix well
